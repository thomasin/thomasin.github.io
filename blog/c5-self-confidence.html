What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

The book states that mindfulness is essentially self awareness. Mindfulness is a practice of paying attention to your environment and emotions in a non judgemental, accepting way. Therefore self awareness, which is being aware and able to step back from emotions is very similar and a natural partner.

Trying body awareness

I really like exercises like these, I'm very impatient so I struggle to finish them and tend to make them a lot shorter than they should be but I think I'm good at accessing sensations and feelings in my body. I liked bringing the positive emotion into me, I felt tingling in my body and warmness like a big pulsing ball in my centre that if I focused I could probably have managed to expand and diffuse over my whole body. Afterwards I definitely felt happier too, it was a very positive experience.

My emotions are not me

I've encountered this practice of seperating emotions from my mind when I get tattooed. I focus my mindfulness and awareness on reminding myself that pain is a chemical reaction and it usually works in lessening it for me. I try seperating my emotions a lot when I'm angry but struggle a lot, I think I need to work on my mindfulness before being able to effectively distance myself from my emotions.
