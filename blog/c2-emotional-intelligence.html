What did you take from this chapter?

This book is so interesting to me, it's putting into words something I've been attempting to achieve and bring to my life for such a long time. I have a really instinctive reaction to things and working to slow that reaction down so I'm responding rather than reacting is something I've always tried to do with varying degrees of success. I think it impacts everything, from your own happiness to your relationships with other people. I love the idea that your brain is trainable. Throughout my life I've used that flexibility often and I really do believe that what this chapter is saying would not only be highly beneficial but also achievable with patience.

How did you find the two minutes of mindfullness?

I've had a lot of stress in my life recently and as a result I feel like my mind is a lot slower, heavier and uninspired at the moment. Two minutes wasn't long enough to get that heaviness out of my head I think I'd need to use longer times or while walking or working out as those are the times I feel lightest.
