How much snapper

Snapper cards are public transport electronic cards used to tag on and off busses and trains in Wellington. I was curious about how much money I was spending on Snapper, so I decided to turn the data from the Snapper website into a graph and some info that would give me more insight into my public transport habits.

I really enjoyed manipulating the data to get the information I wanted and perfecting my d3 line graphing. A challenge was making sure the numbers were accurate so I wrote tests to cover all my manipulation functions to ensure I was getting the right results.
